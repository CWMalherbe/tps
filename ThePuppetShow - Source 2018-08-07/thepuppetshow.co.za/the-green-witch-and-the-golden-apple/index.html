THE GREEN WITCH AND THE GOLDEN APPLE

Ages: 5 – 11 Time: 45 minutes

Cost: R15 per child with a minimum of R 2 500.00 per show.

The stars of “The Green Witch and the Golden Apple”

MJ and the vile Green Witch

Mom and Pete the Pirate

Meet MJ the Pirate and Dazzle her dragon.

M and D Productions first Muppet Show

This exhilarating interactive show absorbs the audience into a journey of discovering the benefits of healthy eating habits. It’s hilarity for the little ones at it’s best, with many contemporary parodies of songs from Lady Gaga’s “Bad Romance’ as the Green witch’s intro song, to MJ’s “Good Food” song to the tune of “Fire Ball”.

MJ the pirate found a treasure map hidden in an old book. Now, MJ and Pete Pirate are on a quest to find the TREASURE OF THE GOLDEN APPLE before the revolting Green Witch gets her hands on it. The Golden Apple is really special because if you have this treasure you’ll never ever get sick again! Come share in MJ and Pete’s adventure as they face all sorts of perils from frozen pirates to Dragons…. and of course the ever-present disgusting Green Witch!

The show is available in English and Afrikaans

The show can be accommodated in most venues. We require an electrical point to plug in our sound system, good ambient lighting, and a 3mx3m HARD SURFACE to work on.
